  • Microclimate Regulation
    We designed a sunshine room,which can fully absorb sunlight with closed windows and doors in winter,and fully ventilate in a open space with opened windows in summer. The photovoltaic power system is out of the main building, in order to facilitate the rapid construction and be conducive to the roof ventilation as well as heat dissipation.At the same time, the sunshine room serves as a harmonious cozy house. We can decorate the interior with enjoyable flowers or plants, then invite some close friends to sit together, admiring the beauty of nature.
  • Air Insulation
    The roof is divided into two layers. The first layer, supported by some brackets laid on the second layer, is covered with photovoltaic solar energy panels. The space between the two layers allows air to flow, providing air currents that benefit the heat dissipation of the solar energy panels in high temperature, ensuring the smooth operation of the solar energy system.The first layer also serve as blinds that shield the house from sunshine, preventing greenhouse effect of the house and thus reducing the consumption of energy.
  • Wall Insulation
    The thermal insulation of the building wall is composed by the polyurethane foam ,which is made of sugar cane fiber. The bagasse is a byproduct of the sugar industry, and most of the sugar mills are directly burned, resulting in a serious of wasted resources. Through a series of reactions, we re-use the bagasse to make good insulation properties of polyurethane insulation as wall materials, which is a realization of re-use of waste materials.
