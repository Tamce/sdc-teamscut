Comunication
  • Space Philosophy
    Three belts with three ecological cores——It is the experiment and practice of long house on architectural space theory.The narrow and long residential homestead,which with a width of 4.8 meters and a depth of 18 meters, is further divided to form a welldefined integrated wall,service belt and living belt with three ecological cores.To precisely plan the appropriate dimensions of equipment space and living space in a long depth.
  • Structure System
    The modular, prefabricated construction system allows longhouses to implement fast, low-impact construction in complex spaces. The team completed the assembly of the prefabricated modules in 2 days and completed the overall construction in 14 days.
  • Passive Ecological Technology
    The morphological unification of active and passive technologies allows green building technology to be effectively implemented in residential buildings.
  • Integration System
    Building equipment system integrates HVAC, photovoltaic, electric power, water supply and drainage, smart home, green ecology and other technical systems, combining with the characteristics of housing space for effective integration and control, system integration to suitability as the goal, to provide a set of efficient and comfortable solutions.
